# Cookie Policy

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Cookies enhance user experience by:

- Remembering your preferences and settings
- Enabling website functionality
- Analyzing website traffic and usage
- Providing personalized content and advertisements
- Enabling social media features

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you which amount to a request for services.

**Purpose**: Website functionality, security, user authentication
**Duration**: Session or up to 1 year
**Examples**: 
- Shopping cart cookies
- Login session cookies
- Security tokens

### Analytical/Performance Cookies

These cookies allow us to recognize and count the number of visitors and see how visitors move around our website. This helps us improve the way our website works.

**Purpose**: Website analytics, performance monitoring, user behavior analysis
**Duration**: Up to 2 years
**Examples**:
- Google Analytics (_ga, _gid)
- Performance monitoring tools

### Functional Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

**Purpose**: Enhanced functionality, personalization, user preferences
**Duration**: Up to 1 year
**Examples**:
- Language preference cookies
- Region selection
- UI customization settings

### Targeting/Advertising Cookies

These cookies are set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other sites.

**Purpose**: Targeted advertising, marketing campaigns, retargeting
**Duration**: Up to 2 years
**Examples**:
- Google AdSense
- Facebook Pixel
- Advertising network cookies

### Social Media Cookies

These cookies are set by a range of social media services that we have added to the site to enable you to share our content with your friends and networks.

**Purpose**: Social media integration, content sharing, social features
**Duration**: Up to 2 years
**Examples**:
- Facebook SDK
- Twitter widgets
- LinkedIn sharing

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

1. **Browser Settings**: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings
2. **Opt-out Tools**: Use industry opt-out tools for advertising cookies
3. **Consent Management**: Use our cookie consent banner to manage preferences

### Browser-Specific Instructions

#### Chrome
1. Click the three dots menu → Settings
2. Click "Privacy and security" → Cookies and other site data
3. Choose your preferred settings

#### Firefox
1. Click the three lines menu → Options
2. Click "Privacy & Security"
3. Adjust cookie settings in the "Cookies and Site Data" section

#### Safari
1. Click "Safari" → "Preferences"
2. Click the "Privacy" tab
3. Select your preferred cookie settings

#### Edge
1. Click the three dots menu → Settings
2. Click "Site permissions" → Cookies and site data
3. Adjust your settings

### Opt-Out Tools

- **Google Analytics**: [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- **Google AdSense**: [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com)
- **Facebook**: [Facebook Ad Preferences](https://www.facebook.com/adpreferences/ad_settings)

## Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may impact your experience on our website:

- **Essential Cookies**: May prevent the website from functioning properly
- **Functional Cookies**: You may need to re-enter information or reconfigure settings
- **Analytical Cookies**: We won't be able to track website usage for improvements
- **Advertising Cookies**: You may see more generic advertisements
